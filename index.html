SparkTalks Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

SparkTalks ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our app handles information.

Data We Collect

SparkTalks does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personal data.

Third-Party Services

SparkTalks does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

SparkTalks is designed for use by families and children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 or any other users. The app functions entirely on-device with no data leaving your device.

In-App Purchases

SparkTalks offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's privacy policy for information on how purchase data is handled.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
